Breaking Down Our Culture

Bump Reference was created by Garrett Berthoumieux, Zachary Love and Zamien Allard shortly after they met. The three discovered they emerged from a similar background: all attend New York City private schools. They bonded on the experiences the three faced being some of the few African Americans let alone minorities at the schools they attended. Rap/trap was very popular amongst the three and their classmates, but in school it was often assumed they knew the everything about "that life" because the three are African Americans. This could be frustrating for people to always be coming to them for lyrical references and knew others could empathize. Therefore Garrett, Zachary and Zamien wanted to create a free service that allows anyone to discover what's behind the lyrics of their favorite songs.
